How to play the Price Is Right!

Game Rules

A Fabulous product wil present itself and you will have to decide what the price is. To keep things simple we are looking for the original Manufacturer Suggested Retail Price.

  • In order to win you must be the closest to the correct price withoutgoing over.
  • Once both PLayers have entered their prices press the GO! button.
  • We will compare your answers and assign a point to the round winner.
  • Press the Next! button to bring up the next product.
  • First player to reach 10 correct answers wins!
